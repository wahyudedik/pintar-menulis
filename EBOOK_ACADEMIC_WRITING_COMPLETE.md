### 2. Akademisi Submit Paper

**Input:**
- Kategori: Academic Writing → Abstract
- Brief: "Penelitian tentang pengaruh social media terhadap kesehatan mental remaja, metode kuantitatif, 500 responden"
- Tone: Formal

**Output AI:**
```
ABSTRACT

Background: The proliferation of social media platforms has raised concerns about their impact on adolescent mental health. Despite extensive research, the specific mechanisms through which social media affects mental well-being remain unclear.

Objective: This study aims to examine the relationship between social media usage patterns and mental health outcomes among Indonesian adolescents aged 15-19 years.

Methods: A cross-sectional quantitative study was conducted with 500 respondents from Jakarta, Bandung, and Surabaya. Data were collected using validated questionnaires measuring social media usage (hours per day, platforms used, engagement types) and mental health indicators (anxiety, depression, self-esteem). Statistical analysis included correlation and multiple regression.

Results: Findings revealed a significant positive correlation between excessive social media use (>4 hours/day) and anxiety symptoms (r=0.45, p<0.001). Instagram and TikTok showed the strongest associations with body image concerns. However, supportive online communities demonstrated protective effects on mental well-being.

Conclusion: While excessive social media use poses risks to adolescent mental health, the quality of online interactions matters more than quantity. Interventions should focus on promoting healthy digital habits and fostering positive online communities.

Keywords: social media, mental health, adolescents, anxiety, depression, digital well-being
```
