## SEO Best Practices yang Diimplementasikan

### 1. Keyword Optimization
- Primary keyword di title, H1, first paragraph
- Secondary keywords di H2, H3
- LSI keywords throughout content
- Natural keyword density (1-2%)

### 2. Content Structure
- Clear heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)
- Bullet points dan numbered lists
- Table of contents untuk long-form

### 3. Readability
- Flesch Reading Ease: 60-70
- Average sentence length: 15-20 words
- Transition words: 30%+
- Active voice: 80%+

### 4. Technical SEO
- Meta description 150-160 characters
- Title tag 50-60 characters
- Image alt text descriptive
- Internal linking 3-5 per article

### 5. User Intent
- Informational: How-to, guides, tutorials
- Navigational: Brand searches, product pages
- Transactional: Reviews, comparisons, best of
- Commercial: Product reviews, buying guides
